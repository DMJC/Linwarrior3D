Utility function to load uncompressed lower-left 24 or 32 bit tga bitmaps.

Note that zipped tga files may be smaller than other common compressed formats when zipped => Distribution size is less than or similar to when png or jpg is used, the installation size is larger but the quality is as good as it gets - assuming 8 bits per pixel. Besides tga is one of the simplest and fastest to load. An enhancement may be to have *.tga.gz if at all.
