Detailed Description

Class that encapsulates the simulation time and animation timing.

Serious question: What time & date format would be used as a common planetary calendar? Is that sensible at all?! So far I assume terran planetary dates everywhere. But time & date should be adjusted to the planet and planet's date shall be mapped like beween maya, gregorian or moon calendars.
