◆ glGetTransposeInverseRotationMatrix()

static void glGetTransposeInverseRotationMatrix ( float *  invm16fv)
inlinestatic

Billboarding Extracts and inverts the rotational part of the MODELVIEW_MATRIX by embedding the transposed upper 3x3 into an otherwise identity 4x4 matrix - assumes that no scaling was applied.

The result can be glMultMatrix'ed in to eliminate rotations for billboarding.
